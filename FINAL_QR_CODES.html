📋 How to Use:

  1. Download Expo Go app on your phone (App Store or Play Store)
  2. Make sure your phone is on the SAME WiFi as this computer
  3. Open Expo Go app
  4. Tap "Scan QR code" in Expo Go
  5. Point your phone at the MOBILE APP QR code (left side)
  6. Wait 30-60 seconds for the app to build and load
  7. Test it! Go to Settings → Test Backend Connection

✅ What You Can Do Now:

In the mobile app:
• Go to Settings → Backend API
• Tap "Test Backend Connection" (should show ✅ Connected)
• Tap "Login / Register"
• Create a new account (saved to real backend!)
• Login with your account
• Check auth status to see your logged-in user
• Use all features: Tasks, Calendar, Focus, Expenses, etc.
